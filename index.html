Logos Lumen

Humanity’s Unburnable Library

Our Vision

Logos Lumen is born from a passion to preserve humanity’s classics. We gather public domain works—freed from copyright as of 2025 for pre-1929 U.S. publications—offering them to all for use, sharing, and inspiration without barriers.

Web 3 Foundation

Built on Web 3’s decentralized promise, we use blockchain and distributed networks to shield these treasures from censorship or loss, ensuring they endure as a shared heritage.

Preservation Through Technology

IPFS stores our works across a global network, keeping them accessible even if parts of the web falter. Dogecoin’s blockchain inscribes their records, creating an eternal, tamper-proof legacy for all to cherish.
