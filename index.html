Logos Lumen

Humanity’s Unburnable Library

Our Mission

Logos Lumen is dedicated to preserving public domain works, those no longer under copyright protection as of 2025 for pre-1929 U.S. publications. These materials are freely available for use, distribution, and adaptation without legal constraints.

Web 3 Infrastructure

Utilizing Web 3 technologies, we employ decentralized systems to protect these works from censorship or data loss, ensuring their availability as a shared cultural resource.

Technological Preservation

IPFS provides distributed storage across a global network, maintaining accessibility even during network disruptions. Dogecoin’s blockchain records references to these works, establishing a permanent, immutable archive.
